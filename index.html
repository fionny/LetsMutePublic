Lets Mute

One hotkey to mute Zoom, Teams, Webex, and Google Meet — no matter which app is in focus.

Download for Mac — Free

macOS 14 Ventura or later  ·  Apple Silicon & Intel

What it does

🎙️

Universal mute hotkey

Press once to mute or unmute any supported app, even when it's in the background.

📅

Meeting countdown

Your next meeting's start time shows in the menu bar so you're never caught off guard.

🔇

Auto-mute on lock

Mutes automatically when you lock your screen or disconnect your headphones.

⚠️

Speaking-while-muted alert

A subtle warning when you're talking but your mic is muted.

🎥

Camera control

Toggle your camera on/off with a separate hotkey alongside mute.

🔒

Completely private

No data leaves your device. No analytics, no accounts, no cloud.

Supported apps

Works with the apps you use

Zoom  ·  Microsoft Teams  ·  Webex  ·  Google Meet

Lets Mute reads and controls each app's native mute button — no virtual audio devices, no system hacks.
